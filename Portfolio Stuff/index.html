About Me

I started out wanting to pursue a Bachelors in web and graphic design. I taught myself how to do very simple things with CS3 and was constantly using it in my free time. In high school, I took an introduction to multimedia class and got through it fairly easily. My first semester of college, I took a similar class and found that my heart was no longer in web and graphic design. Three years later, I was introduced to SeedPaths I joined the program, unsure if I would enjoy it. After the first day, I knew this was something I wanted as a career.
